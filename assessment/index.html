What It Is

A Map of Where You Actually Are

Most people seeking support already know something is off. What they don't always know is where, or why, or what's connected to what.

The Helios Insight Engine is a structured diagnostic built on the Helios Method — our integrative framework for understanding human wellbeing as a system of interconnected areas, not a set of isolated problems. The assessment guides you through a series of reflective questions across 8 life domains. It takes about 10 minutes. You don't need an account. There's no payment involved.

What you receive at the end is a personalized diagnostic report: a visual map of your current wellbeing, a breakdown of where your energy is flowing and where it's being drained, and initial reflections on the patterns the assessment surface. It's designed to give you something genuinely useful whether or not you ever book a session with us.

The 8 Life Areas

A Whole-Person View of Wellbeing

The Helios Method holds that wellbeing is not a single dimension — it is a field. What shows up in one area is almost always affecting others.

Career & Purpose

Are you doing work that uses your best capacities? Does it align with what you actually value — or has the gap between your work and your sense of meaning quietly widened?

Relationships & Connection

The quality of your close relationships — partner, family, friendship — is one of the most consistent predictors of long-term wellbeing. This area surfaces where connection is nourishing and where it is costing you.

Health & Body

Sleep, movement, energy, physical vitality. The body is not separate from the mind — it is the medium through which everything else is experienced.

Mind & Mental Wellbeing

Anxiety, mood, rumination, mental clarity, the quality of your inner life. This area often has the most immediate relationship to the others.

Finances & Security

Financial stress is one of the most pervasive and undertreated contributors to psychological distress. This area explores your relationship to money, security, and financial autonomy.

Home & Environment

The physical and social environment you inhabit shapes your mood, behavior, and nervous system in ways that are easy to overlook until you change them.

Growth & Learning

The human need to develop — to learn, to be challenged, to expand — is as real as the need for rest. Stagnation is rarely inert; it tends to erode.

Play & Joy

Often the first thing dropped when life gets demanding, and often a reliable early indicator of something being off. This area asks whether you are actually living, or just managing.

After the Assessment

Your Report, and What's Next

The Insight Engine delivers your personalized report immediately on completion — no email required, no waiting.

Your report shows your scores across all 8 life areas in a visual format, identifies the domains showing the most tension or depletion, and offers initial reflections on what the pattern might mean. It is designed to be read as a whole, not just by individual score.

Some people use the report on its own — as a starting point for personal reflection, journaling, or a conversation with someone they trust. Others bring it to a free consultation with Francesca or Nicholas, which gives us an immediate shared foundation for understanding what's going on and what kind of support might help.

There is no obligation to book anything. The assessment is free, and so is the first conversation about it. We built it because we believe the work of understanding yourself should start before a financial commitment — not after.

Privacy

Your assessment results are private. No account is required. Sharing your results with a Helios practitioner is entirely optional — you can book a consultation and discuss what came up without ever transmitting the raw data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
