Francesca Licciardi

MA, LMHC, LPCC · Licensed Mental Health Counselor

Individual & Couples Therapy · Telehealth Available

Verified by Psychology Today

19 years guiding people
toward inner alignment

Francesca Licciardi brings a rare combination of intellectual rigor and deep human warmth to every therapeutic relationship. Her insatiable curiosity about the human psyche and genuine appreciation for all people gives her the energy to accompany each client on their unique path toward self-actualization and inner peace.

From anxiety, depression, and PTSD to low motivation, life transitions, and relationship disconnect — Francesca works with the full spectrum of human experience. Her approach is never one-size-fits-all; each care plan is customized to the unique makeup of the individual or couple she's working with.

For couples, Francesca draws on her certification in Imago Therapy — a powerful method for developing effective communication and opening intimacy through emotional awareness. For individuals, she weaves together somatic work, CBT, hypnotherapy, Internal Family Systems, and more into a cohesive, integrated path forward.

Nicholas Tucker

Integrative Coach & Care Coordinator

Integrative Coaching · Hypnotherapy · Care Coordination

A systems-aware approach
to whole-person growth

Nicholas has been coaching since 2017 — first as an internal coach within high-growth software companies, and now professionally with individuals navigating transitions, relational challenges, and personal growth. Trained in the Co-Active coaching model (CTI), Organization & Relationship Systems Coaching (ORSC by CRR Global), and Positive Intelligence (PQ), he brings a systems-aware, whole-person approach to every engagement. He partners with clients to identify and quiet their inner saboteurs, cultivate self-command, and move from self-judgment toward genuine self-love — building actionable strategies for lasting change across both personal and professional life.
