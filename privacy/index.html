Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Helios Integrative Health ("Helios," "we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website and the confidentiality of clients in our care. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information in connection with this website (heliosintegrativehealth.com) and our associated services.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of this website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you submit a contact or booking form on this website, we collect the information you enter — including your name, email address, and any message content you choose to share. This information is transmitted via Netlify Forms and stored securely by Netlify, Inc. We use it solely to respond to your inquiry and, if appropriate, to schedule an initial consultation.

The Helios Insight Engine

The Helios Insight Engine is a separate web application hosted at insight-engine.heliosintegrativehealth.com. Your assessment responses are stored within that application only. Sharing your assessment results with a Helios practitioner is entirely optional and occurs only with your explicit action. Refer to the Insight Engine's own privacy notice for details specific to that application.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, our hosting provider (Netlify) may collect basic server log information — IP addresses, browser type, referring URLs, and page access times — for security and operational purposes. We do not use this information to identify individual visitors and do not sell it to third parties.

We do not currently use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) on this website. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

How We Use Information

We use the information you submit through contact forms to:

  • Respond to your inquiry within 24 business hours
  • Schedule and conduct a complimentary initial consultation
  • Determine whether our services are appropriate for your needs

We do not use contact form submissions for marketing, do not add you to mailing lists without explicit consent, and do not share your information with third parties except as described below.

Clinical Confidentiality

Client information in the context of therapy or clinical services is governed by professional ethical standards, HIPAA (where applicable), and applicable state law — not solely by this website privacy policy. Francesca Licciardi maintains clinical records through SimplePractice, a HIPAA-compliant electronic health records and telehealth platform. SimplePractice's privacy practices are governed by its own Business Associate Agreement and privacy policy.

Coaching relationships are confidential as a matter of professional ethics but are not subject to HIPAA requirements. Nicholas Tucker maintains client information with appropriate security measures.

Third-Party Services

This website uses the following third-party services:

  • Netlify — website hosting and form processing. Netlify may process data in the United States. See Netlify's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — typography. Google may receive your IP address when fonts are loaded. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • SimplePractice — used for scheduling, telehealth sessions, and clinical records management. See SimplePractice's Privacy Policy.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. Our hosting provider (Netlify) may set functional cookies for form security and bot prevention. No marketing or advertising cookies are used.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our website, please contact us at the address below.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information you have submitted to us through our contact form. To make such a request, please email us at hello@heliosintegrativehealth.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
